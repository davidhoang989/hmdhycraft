Who We Are

About HMD Strategy & Export

“We bridge the gap between Vietnam's traditional craftsmanship and the demands of modern global retail.”

HMD Strategy & Export Company Limited sources and exports handcrafted natural-fiber products from Vietnam's Mekong Delta. We work closely with skilled artisan communities, weaving villages that have refined their craft over generations, and turn that craftsmanship into retail-ready products for international buyers. Rooted in the trade since 2019, HMD pairs deep local expertise with a global outlook, and understands what retail chains, e-commerce platforms, and home décor importers need: consistency, flexibility, and the ability to scale.

Our Materials

Water Hyacinth Lục bình

Grown in the rivers and canals of the Mekong Delta, then sun-dried and handwoven into a warm, sturdy weave. Naturally tan, and easily finished in brown wash, black wash, or white.

Seagrass Cói

A fine, rope-like fiber with a smooth, cooler tone. Tightly woven so trays and baskets hold their shape. A distinct material from grey sedge, with its own look and feel.

Buyer FAQ

What buyers usually ask

The wholesale questions we answer most often. Anything else, write to us at joy@hmdhycraft.com.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
MOQ depends on the product line and material. For standard designs in our Storage Baskets Collection, we typically start from 300 to 500 units per SKU. Mixed containers with several SKUs are welcome; for a full quote we will confirm MOQ against the exact items in your enquiry.
What is your lead time from order confirmation?
Standard lead time is 45 to 60 days for a full container of standard designs, counted from deposit and approved pre-production samples. Larger or customised programs are quoted per project.
Do you handle OEM and private label?
Yes. We work with buyers who need private label goods for retail: custom dimensions, custom weave patterns, buyer-supplied colour references, buyer-branded hangtags, care labels, and retail packaging. All artwork and packaging is produced against your approved artwork files.
How does sampling work?
We prepare paid samples matched to your reference photos, tech-pack, or physical sample. Sampling lead time is 10 to 15 days depending on complexity. Sample fees are typically credited back against a confirmed production order.
What are your payment terms?
Standard terms are 30 percent deposit against proforma invoice and 70 percent balance before B/L release, by T/T. For repeat buyers we can discuss L/C at sight or open terms on a case-by-case basis.
How is quality controlled before shipment?
Every unit is inspected by hand at the village and again at our drying and packing facility: pattern and dimension against approved samples, moisture kept below 15 percent to prevent mould during ocean freight, and an eco-friendly anti-mould treatment on every piece. Third-party inspection by SGS, TÜV, or buyer-nominated firms is arranged on request.
Do you organise container loading and freight?
Yes. We consolidate at our HCMC facility and load 20' and 40'HQ containers to buyer specifications: cartons per SKU, palletisation, load plans, and photo and video documentation. FOB Ho Chi Minh City is our default incoterm; CIF and DAP are quoted on request.
What certifications and compliance do you support?
Our baskets are handwoven natural fibre goods (HS 4602). Phytosanitary certificates and Certificate of Origin (Form B, Form E, or Form EUR.1 depending on destination) are issued through Vietnam Customs. Our anti-mould treatment is compliant with import regulations for the EU, UK, US, Canada, Australia, and Japan.
