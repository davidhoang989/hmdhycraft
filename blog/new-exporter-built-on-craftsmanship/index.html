A new exporter, built on years of craftsmanship behind the scenes

14 May 2026·2 min read

When people hear "new exporter", they often think of limited experience. In our case, the story is a little different.

HMD Water Hyacinth Craft was recently established as an export-focused company. Behind it is a network of skilled weaving groups in Đồng Tháp, Vietnam, many of whom have been working on export products for years.

For a long time, these artisans worked quietly behind the scenes, producing for different exporters. Today, we are bringing them together under one system.

Not just to produce. To build something more consistent, scalable, and aligned with international markets.

Because one of the biggest challenges with handmade products is not craftsmanship. It is consistency.

What we organise

  • Standardised processes across every weaving group we work with.
  • Aligned quality so a basket woven in one village looks and feels the same as one woven in another.
  • Reliable delivery for every order, with production planned before your season hits.

So buyers do not have to choose between "handmade" and "reliable".

You can have all three:

Handmade craftsmanship
+ Consistent quality
+ Scalable production

That is the direction we are building.

If you work on natural material products, or you are exploring new sourcing options from Vietnam, we would be glad to connect and exchange ideas.
