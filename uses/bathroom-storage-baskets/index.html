Bathroom Storage Baskets — Wholesale Supplier from Vietnam

Nested handwoven sets for retail shelves, hotel amenity carts, and gift assortments. Water hyacinth and seagrass, woven in Đồng Tháp, consolidated FOB Ho Chi Minh City.

Buyers looking for bathroom baskets share three problems: the design has to sit well next to porcelain and glass, the fibre has to breathe in a humid room, and the container has to arrive with enough retail-ready packs to earn its shelf space back. This page is a shortlist for exactly that.

What buyers ask for on this category

  • Nested sets, not single pieces. One SKU that becomes three or four sizes on the shelf. Lower freight per unit, cleaner display, easier to price.
  • Colour that reads warm under bathroom LEDs. Natural water hyacinth and undyed seagrass photograph reliably in-store and online. Brown wash and black finishes are available.
  • Weave that survives handling. Fuller water hyacinth for structured storage, finer seagrass for trays and lidded sets. Both hold shape after the third handling on a shop floor.
  • Liners optional, not shouted. Cotton or linen liners quietly protect the fibre from splashes without turning the basket into a soft-goods product.

Frequently asked

Are water hyacinth baskets safe for a humid bathroom?
Yes, when kept out of direct water contact. Water hyacinth breathes well and does not trap moisture the way a plastic bin does, so damp towels vent instead of stagnating. HMD baskets are dried and cured before weaving; buyers who plan to place stock near a bath edge should add a fabric liner (available as an option) to protect the fibre from splashes.
Do the sets nest for retail shelving?
Yes. Each set is nested and cartoned as one SKU for shelf display and lower freight. Nested sets keep shelf footprint compact while offering the buyer three sizes in one purchase. The retailer receives one carton per set and can either sell nested or split for display.
What is the MOQ for a mixed container of bathroom baskets?
MOQ is 300 to 500 units per SKU depending on the design. Mixed containers with two to five SKUs are welcome and are the most common first order. For a 20-foot container we typically consolidate three to four SKUs; for a 40-foot high-cube, five to eight.
What is the HS code for bathroom storage baskets?
HS 4602 covers basketwork and other articles made from plaiting materials. HMD invoices as 4602.19.90 for handwoven storage baskets in water hyacinth and seagrass. Buyers in the EU and UK typically clear at zero duty under GSP; the HMD invoice includes a Form A certificate of origin on request.
Can I private label the packaging?
Yes. HMD prints buyer-branded hangtags, care cards, and inner cartons at additional charge. Barcodes (EAN/UPC) are printed as supplied. Minimum for private-label packaging is one full SKU run; below that, packaging is neutral with a small HMD care card inside.
What is the lead time from PO to FOB Ho Chi Minh City?
Standard designs from the Storage Baskets Collection ship in six to eight weeks from confirmed PO. Custom shapes or new colourways add two to three weeks for sampling. Container consolidation and loading at HCMC take an additional three to five working days after production is complete.
