Planter Baskets for Hotel & Cafe Interiors — Wholesale from Vietnam

Tall floor planters and mid-height decorative baskets for hotel lobbies, restaurants, and cafe interiors. Seagrass and water hyacinth, woven in Đồng Tháp, consolidated FOB Ho Chi Minh City.

Hospitality planters sit under strong lights, take a weekly water top-up, and must not distract from the room. The specification that matters is not the pattern — it is the interior fit for a standard nursery pot, the top-line that reads clean at seated eye level, and a fibre that ages evenly. This page is a shortlist for exactly that.

What buyers ask for on this category

  • Interior sized to standard nursery pots. 18-inch and 20-inch top diameters that fit the pots most FF&E schedules already specify. Custom interior diameters available at sampling for signature designs.
  • Top-line that reads clean at seated eye level. Rolled rim or subtle chevron finish rather than raw cut ends. This is where a photograph either works or looks amateur in the design magazine feature.
  • Seagrass for structure, water hyacinth for tone. Seagrass holds a tight vertical wall in tall pieces without a metal frame. Water hyacinth reads warmer and works better in daylight lobbies. Both are used across the collection.
  • Removable plastic liner insert. Every planter ships with a discreet liner insert so the housekeeping team can water the plant without the basket getting damp. Insert is neutral black or clear, buyer-branded on request.

Container maths

A 20-foot container typically carries 180–240 tall floor planters across two to three SKUs — tall pieces do not nest so the count is lower than storage baskets. A 40-foot high-cube typically carries 450–560 tall planters. A mixed load with medium storage baskets on top improves the container yield.

Frequently asked

Can the baskets sit outdoors?
Not directly. Water hyacinth and seagrass are indoor fibres. On a covered veranda or a lit atrium the pieces perform well; direct rain or sprinkler contact will shorten the life of the fibre. HMD's outdoor-treated line uses a synthetic weave and is a separate SKU family — enquire if outdoor pieces are the requirement.
Do the planters ship with an insert?
Yes. Every planter SKU ships with a neutral black plastic liner insert sized to the basket interior. Liners are recyclable and can be replaced separately. Buyer-branded inserts are available on runs above 200 units per SKU.
What is the MOQ for a hotel FF&E programme?
MOQ is 200 to 300 units per SKU for tall floor pieces (lower than storage baskets because unit price is higher). A first hospitality order typically confirms two SKUs across two heights to cover lobby, corridor, and guest-room zones. Custom sizes for a signature lobby moment are available at sampling.
Can you match a supplied colour or finish reference?
Yes. Send a physical swatch or a hi-res photograph and HMD samples against it at a two-week lead time. Common hospitality finishes are natural, black wash, and grey wash. Metallic and painted finishes are quoted separately.
Do you supply care and installation notes for housekeeping?
Yes. Every hospitality shipment includes a one-page care sheet in English and Vietnamese covering watering, dusting, and rotation. Digital copies for the FF&E binder are supplied on request.
What is the lead time from PO to FOB HCMC?
Standard hospitality designs ship in six to eight weeks from confirmed PO. Custom interior diameters or a signature colourway add two to three weeks for sampling. Container consolidation adds three to five working days after production.
