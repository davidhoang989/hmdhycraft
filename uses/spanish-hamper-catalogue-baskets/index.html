Hamper Baskets for Spanish Gift Catalogues — Wholesale Supplier from Vietnam

Trays, lidded sets, and rectangular hampers sized for two-bottle wine, gourmet grazing, and Christmas assortments. Spanish-language private label, Christmas timing calendar, FOB Ho Chi Minh City to Barcelona and Valencia.

A Spanish gift-catalogue buyer plans against one calendar: the artwork closes in July, the container has to reach Barcelona or Valencia by early November, the hamper assembly runs through November, and the last box ships to a retail customer on 24 December. The basket has to arrive on time, sit right for a two-bottle wine set plus a wrapper, and carry Spanish-language private-label print. This page is a shortlist for exactly that calendar.

What buyers ask for on this category

  • Interior sized to a two-bottle wine plus wrapper. The Spanish hamper standard is a two-bottle wine set with a folded wrapper and one or two gourmet items. Interior footprint 11–13 inches wide covers the working case. HMD-024 and HMD-031 both fit that brief; custom interior sizes for signature hampers quote at sampling.
  • Spanish-language private-label print. Hangtags, care cards, and inner cartons printed in Spanish (or Catalan, Galician, Euskera on request). Buyer-supplied artwork accepted; HMD's print partner in HCMC handles the run.
  • Christmas timing calendar hard-locked. For a Spanish Christmas retail push, confirm PO by early July. Standard designs ship six to eight weeks; ocean transit HCMC to Barcelona is roughly 28–34 days; add 5–7 days customs and last-mile to the assembly warehouse. October arrival at hamper assembly is the standard target.
  • Weave tight enough for small items in the base. Water hyacinth and seagrass are woven tightly for gift work so a wrapped truffle or a small ceramic does not slip through the base. Loose ends are trimmed at the QC line.

Container maths

A first Spanish-hamper container typically consolidates three to five SKUs across tray and lidded shapes to cover a full gift-catalogue assortment. A 40-foot high-cube carries 1,200–1,500 nested sets and reaches Barcelona in 28–34 days from HCMC B/L, Valencia in 26–32 days. October arrival at the buyer's hamper assembly warehouse is the standard target for a Christmas retail push.

Frequently asked

What is the timing calendar for a Spanish Christmas programme?
Confirm PO by early July for a Christmas retail push. Standard designs ship six to eight weeks from PO; ocean transit HCMC to Barcelona is 28–34 days, Valencia 26–32 days; add 5–7 days customs and last-mile to the assembly warehouse. Target arrival at hamper assembly: mid to end October. Buyers who confirm earlier get first pick of container slots.
Do you print private-label artwork in Spanish?
Yes. Hangtags, care cards, and inner cartons print in Spanish, Catalan, Galician, or Euskera as required. Buyer-supplied artwork accepted; HMD's print partner in HCMC handles the run. Minimum for private-label print is one full SKU run.
Which Spanish ports do you consolidate for?
Default FOB is Ho Chi Minh City; the buyer's freight forwarder handles Spanish entry. Common Spanish arrival ports for HMD shipments are Barcelona and Valencia; Bilbao and Algeciras are quoted on request. CIF and DAP are quoted on request — DAP requires the buyer's EORI number for customs clearance.
What is the MOQ for a Spanish hamper programme?
MOQ is 300 to 500 units per SKU. A first Spanish hamper programme typically confirms three to four SKUs to cover two-bottle wine, gourmet grazing, and one signature shape. Repeat annual buyers get priority container slots and hold their tooling across seasons at no extra cost.
Can we get a matching cotton liner for the hamper?
Yes. Cream, kraft, and buyer-branded print liners are available as an add-on SKU sized to the basket interior. Minimum liner run is 200 units per colour. Liners are typically bagged separately for the hamper assembly team, not pre-inserted.
What is the HS code and duty position for Spanish entry?
HS 4602.19.90 covers handwoven basketwork. Spanish importers clear at zero duty under EU GSP+ for Vietnamese origin (HMD invoice includes Form A on request). VAT applies at Spanish rates on entry; the buyer's freight forwarder handles the paperwork.
